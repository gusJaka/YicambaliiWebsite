How to Book

1
Find Equipment

To find the equipment you want to rent, you just have to simply go to the browse equipment menu. In the Browse Equipment menu, a list of available equipment that is available for rent will be displayed. You can also search specific equipment you want to find by typing it in the search bar.

2
Checkout

To check out the equipment you have found and add to your cart, you can go directly to the check-out menu, and a list of equipment you have added to your cart will be shown. The subtotal is also shown and you can simply click the checkout button.

3
Payment

The payment of the equipment rented will be done when you pick up the camera in our office or when you meet up with our staff in a designed area by cash. The equipment should be paid for when picked up not when returning it. Payment also can be done through bank transfer.
