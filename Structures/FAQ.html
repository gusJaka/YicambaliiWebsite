FAQ (Frequently Asked Question)

1
New User
What is Yicambalii?

Yicambalii is a dedicated photo and video equipment rental service founded in 2019 in Bali (Indonesia). Providing a variety of cameras, lenses, and accessories for your hobbies and work needs, at affordable prices and service from the heart for you. We are growing better every day and aim to be the best photo and video equipment rental service you can rely on.

How to Order

Ordering equipment can come directly to Yicambalii at your City Branch and if you can't come to the place directly, we will help with the process of ordering tools via Whatsapp, BBM, SMS, or Online Chat. If your order has been processed by our Online Customer Service, you can pick up the equipment according to the time and location of your order in Yicambalii that you want, and cannot be represented by someone else. For direct rental, you can visit Yicambalii and will be assisted directly by our Customer Service for the process of borrowing the equipment. You can deposit one of your valid and valid identities at Yicambalii at the time of collection.

Do we have to become a member?

No! you don't have to become a member to be able to rent equipment or services at Yicambalii. You simply can do check out on our website, come to our offices, or contact us through our social media.

Order
What if i cancel the order?

If you cancel an equipment rental order or change equipment rental suddenly (less than 1x24 (one time twenty four) hours from the schedule for ordering or picking up equipment), the Tenant is willing and obliged to pay a cancellation fee of Rp. 50% (fifty percent) of the equipment rental price per day, for each canceled equipment. Payment is made no later than 1x24 (one time twenty-four) hours after the cancellation of the equipment rental.

How do i order through Whatsapp?

To order through whatsapp, you can simply state the equipment you want to rent, your name, your address, date of rent, and what is the equipment will be used for. It is the same for all of our social media.

How do i reschedule?

If the you wants to make additional equipment rentals, the you is required to confirm and seek prior approval from Yicambalii. Yicambalii has the right not to give approval to extend the rental of the equipment if the equipment is ordered by the next you. If the you is unable to return the equipment rented for any reason and causes Yicambalii to provide a different tool with a different rental price for the equipment to the next you, the you offers and pays to pay the rental price for the equipment offered to the next you.

3
Equipment Return
Can I return the equipment using Go-Jek?

We do not advice you to return or pick up the equipment using Go-Jek for the concern of our equipment. If the equipment rented has damage when returned, the customer is 100% responsible for the penalty.

What if i returned it late?

If the customer is late in returning the equipment that has been rented, the customer is willing and obliged to pay the overtime fee with the following illustration:
- Late 3 (three) – 6 (six) hours : There will be a fee of 25% (twenty five percent) of the total rental price per day
- Late 6 (six) – 12 (twelve) hours : 50% (fifty percent) of the total rental price per day will be charged
- Lateness of more than 12 (twelve) hours : 100% (one hundred percent) will be charged (accounting for 1 (one) day rental)

When should i return the equipment?

The calculation of the duration of the loan period and the return of equipment at Yicambalii is every 24 (twenty four) hours.
