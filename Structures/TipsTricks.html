Tips and Trick about Camera

1
Get a Good Camera Bag

Having a good camera bag is important for the protection of the camera, especially when the camera is carried around for traveling. It should not only look good, but fit nicely around your shoulders and also have easy access to the camera. There should be space for lenses, battery, filters, etc in your bag camera. Moreover, camera bag can be used to protect the camera inside from friction. This function is very helpful so that the camera is not easily scratched like when placed in any place without protection. One of the important advantages and benefits of using a digital camera bag is that it can protect it from water. Usually, the special camera bag is made of water-resistant so if it rains in the middle of the road, you don't have to worry because the camera is well protected. One of the things that are unavoidable when having a digital camera is that it will fall at any time. If you don't use a camera bag, when it falls it will directly hit the camera and make it a problem.

2
Take the Batteries Out

Batteries are a fairly trivial accessory, but they are also important because they are directly related to photographing. Why we have to remove the battery from the camera while not using is because if it is not removed the battery from the camera or charger when not in use, if it is installed in the camera, the power will slowly decrease. Store the battery in a cool and dry place with silica gel, avoid hot or humid places
