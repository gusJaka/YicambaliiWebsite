Term and Condition

1
Main Provision

The customer guarantees that all data that has been provided to Yicambalii is data that is honest and can be legally accounted for, and is willing and obliged to follow all the procedures and conditions that apply at Yicambalii. Yicambalii is allowed to use the data provided by the customer for Yicambalii's needs.
  2. If the Yicambalii party declares that the customer does not fulfill the data verification process and the terms of the Yicambalii lease, then the Yicambalii party has the right to refuse to lend the equipment to the customer/prospective customer. Yicambalii's decision is absolute and cannot be contested.
  3. The customer is responsible for maintaining the condition of all equipment that is being borrowed during the rental period until the equipment is returned. The lessee is obliged to return the rented equipment in good condition (physically and functionally) as well as complete as when the equipment was taken.
  4. Rental payments are made in full (100%; one hundred percent) at the beginning when picking up the equipment or when you want to carry out other types of transactions, such as when extending the lease and so on.
  5. If the pick-up or return of the equipment is carried out by a third party appointed by the customer, the customer REMAINS fully responsible for the equipment rental transaction, including in the event of damage or loss of the equipment.
2
Rent Procedure
  1. Ordering tools can come directly to Yicambalii at your City Branch and if you can't come to the place directly, we will help to process the tool order via Whatsapp, BBM, SMS, or Online Chat, we do not recommend placing an order by phone call. Or you can rent directly by coming to Yicambalii at the City Branch you are in. For more information, please contact our Online Customer Service.
  2. If your order has been processed by our Online Customer Service, you can pick up the equipment according to the time and location of your order in Yicambalii that you want, and cannot be represented by someone else. For direct rental, you can visit Yicambalii and will be assisted directly by our Customer Service for the process of borrowing the equipment. You can deposit one of your valid and valid identities at Yicambalii at the time of collection.

    Make sure to both check the function of the equipment and the completeness of the equipment that you will rent with our officers before the equipment will be used, to reduce unwanted things at the location during use.
  3. Perfect your activities and capture your precious moments with the equipment you have rented at Yicambalii. If you experience problems with the equipment you rent when you use the location, don't hesitate to ask us, because as soon as possible we will help solve the problems you are experiencing.
  4. After the equipment is finished, you can return the equipment that has been borrowed to Yicambalii. Thank you for entrusting your equipment needs to Yicambalii.
3
Cancel Order
  1. If you cancel an equipment rental order or change equipment rental suddenly (less than 1x24 (one time twenty four) hours from the schedule for ordering or picking up equipment), the customer is willing and obliged to pay a cancellation fee of Rp. 50% (fifty percent) of the equipment rental price per day, for each canceled equipment. Payment is made no later than 1x24 (one time twenty-four) hours after the cancellation of the equipment rental.
  2. If there is no confirmation/news from the Tenant 3 (three) hours after the scheduled time for picking up the equipment, Yicambalii has the right to unilaterally cancel the equipment order and lease the equipment back to another customer. The customer is willing and obligated to pay a cancellation fee of 50% (fifty percent) of the equipment rental price per day, for each equipment deemed void of rental. Payment is made no later than 1x24 (one time twenty-four) hours after the cancellation of the equipment rental.
  3. If the equipment has been taken and the rental period has been running for more than 1 (one) hour and the customer cancels the rental for any reason, then Yicambalii is not obligated to return the rent that has been paid.
4
Pick-Up and Return the Equipment
The equipment rented can be picked-up by the customer at a place agreed by the customer and Yicambalii, or can be picked up by customer at Yicambalii. We do not recommend the return process by online courier services, etc., all risks that occur remain the full responsibility of the customer.
5
Return Delay
  1. The calculation of the duration of the rent period and the return of equipment at Yicambalii is per 24 (twenty four) hours.
  2. The calculation of the start of the rental period is from the time the tool is ordered or the time it is picked up, whichever comes first. If the Tenant picks up the equipment later (late) than the time for ordering the equipment, the start of the rental period will still start from the time the equipment was ordered. However, if the Tenant picks up the tools earlier (early) than the time for ordering the tools, the rental period starts from the time when the tools are picked up.
  3. If the customer wishes to extend the rental of the equipment, the customer is required to confirm and seek prior approval from Yicambalii. Yicambalii has the right not to give approval to extend the rental of the equipment if the equipment has been ordered by the next customer. If the customer is unable to return the equipment being rented for any reason and causes Yicambalii to provide a different tool with a higher rental price for the equipment to the next customer, the customer is willing and obligated to pay the difference in the rental price of the equipment offered to the next Tenant.
  4. If the customer is late in returning the equipment that has been rented, the customer is willing and obliged to pay the overtime fee with the following illustration:
    • Late 3 (three) – 6 (six) hours : There will be a fee of 25% (twenty five percent) of the total rental price per day
    • Late 6 (six) – 12 (twelve) hours : 50% (fifty percent) of the total rental price per day will be charged
    • Lateness of more than 12 (twelve) hours : 100% (one hundred percent) will be charged (accounting for 1 (one) day rental)
6
Equipment Damage and Loss
  1. In the event of damage and/or loss of tools or other equipment (accessories) during the rental period, the borrower is MANDATORY to replace with similar and similar tools or accessories that can be accepted in accordance with (QC/Quality Check) appropriateness of Yicambalii tool quality standards or with replacement in the form of nominal with the latest market price reference. Replacement or payment is made no later than 1x24 (one time twenty-four) hours from the return of the tool.
  2. Customer are PROHIBITED to pawn, sell, lease back, transfer Yicambalii's equipment intentionally or unintentionally to third parties. If the customer continues to do the above and there is damage or loss of equipment, the customer is still RESPONSIBLE and obliged to fully indemnify all the equipment that has been rented from Yicambalii in accordance with (article 6) above.
  3. If the customer intentionally or unintentionally modifies, changes the software system (software) of Yicambalii equipment without the knowledge and approval of Yicambalii, then the customer remains RESPONSIBLE and obliged to fully indemnify the Yicambalii equipment which is damaged or has an abnormal function as at the time of tool loan.
  4. If the customer intentionally or unintentionally damages and/or releases the company identity such as barcode, logo sticker or any other identity related to the Yicambalii company identity, the Tenant is willing and obliged to pay a replacement fee of Rp. 25,000 (twenty five thousand rupiah) for each identity is damaged / loose.
